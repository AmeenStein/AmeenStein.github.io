Economy of Bilboville

In the modern world Bilboville acts for a hub of Inovation due to its amazing educational system and its large government subsedies coming from the National inovation fund. Bilboville Population growth has also been a large factor in its economic growth. Being the European contry with the most imigrants (proportional to its own population). Bilboville still has the lowest taxes in europe having a 5% corporate income tax. This brings many companies to bilboville with many silicon valley companies having their headquarters in bilboville.

Early Years

Bilbovilles Economy for a very long time consisted only of basic shipping a small scale agriculture. The shipping part of its Economy was made possible by its stratgic location off the coast of Spain. But Even with the Shipping at the Bibloville port, Bilbovilles Economy was relativly small.

Industrial Revoloution

During the industrial Revoloution Bilboville Become a hub for trade between France, Great Britan and Spain. This led to rapid industrialization. During this time experienced quite rapid Economic growth.​

Franco-era

During Francos rule over Spain, Spain was hit with many sanctions which meant it needed many products that it couldn’t produce domestically. But Bilbovilles large Industrial base was able to jump in and help Spain with Industrial products. This let many Bilboville companies have a complete monopoly over the Spanish market. Once the Franco regime fell Bilbovilles companies where large enough to compete internationaly.​

Modern Day 1975-now

Nowadays Bilbovilles Economy is still dominated by many Franco-era Companies. These companies are international giants in their fields this in combination with their large port gives them a very strong export-led economy.​
